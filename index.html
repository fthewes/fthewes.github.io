About Me

I am interested in the physics of complex systems in and out of equilibrium. In particular, I’m fascinated by the collective behaviour – both steady-state properties and spatial dynamics - arising from complex interactions between many degrees of freedom. I approach these problems using theoretical tools such as statistical field theories, random matrices, path integrals, and test predictions using numerical methods. Recently, I am interested in effectively controlling these systems at minimal dissipation.

Research

My current projects include:

  • Phase separation with non-local interactions
  • Tracer dynamics in non-equilibrium multicomponent mixtures
  • Stochastic thermodynamics of multicomponent field-theories
